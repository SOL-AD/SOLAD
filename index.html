ABOUT US

Immac Printing and Publishing House is a proudly Filipino-owned company dedicated to producing high-quality, inspiring, and educational printed materials. Established in Taytay, Rizal, Immac has become a trusted name in the field of book publishing, printing, and distribution, serving schools, authors, and institutions across the country.

Our mission is to empower minds through knowledge and creativity. We believe that every book has the power to shape lives, and our goal is to make learning accessible by providing well-designed, affordable, and relevant publications that meet the needs of students, educators, and readers nationwide.

With a team of passionate professionals, state-of-the-art printing facilities, and a commitment to excellence, Immac Printing and Publishing House continues to deliver products that combine quality, integrity, and innovation.
